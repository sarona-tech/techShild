How does it work?
TechShield Reverse-Fund uses an adaptive model to invest. We leverage Sarona VC's network to select 6 VC-backed, top-performing Israeli startups that meet our investment criteria and terms. We screened hundreds of companies to maintain a pool of startups that cohere to our strict criteria. During the fundraising period, we continuously assess the performance of these companies to ensure only the best are included. Our goal is to enable pro-Israel investors to invest in high-growth startups, secure their investments, and optimize returns. By making smart investments, we aim to support Israel's tech industry.
01
Raised Seed, Series-A or later, with notable VC
•
To identify startups most suited for our program, TechShield meticulously established criteria.
•
We focus on promising startups that can thrive in spite of the war, with amazing teams and products.
•
A significant number of Israeli companies apply to join the program, providing comprehensive financial data, team information, and an overview of the company's current status.
•
TechShield uses an adaptive model with pre and post selected companies. We conduct strict assessments of their performance during fundraising and adjust the portfolio accordingly, with agreed terms.
02
Our ‘Reverse-fund’ Investment strategy
•
TechShield uses Reverse-Funds (SPVs) to ensure clarity and confidence for investors by pre-selecting outstanding Israeli startups, securing agreed terms with all of them, and seeking investments only afterward.
•
Reverse-Funds allow investing in a defined group of excellent startups that would be otherwise unavailable to investors.
•
TechShield carefully invites selected investors to join, ensuring every investor shares TechShield's mission and values, creating a strong, united group to support these companies.
•
Investments range from $50k to $1m, allowing a wide range of investors to participate and make a significant impact.
03
Israel’s economic stability and the future
•
The purpose of TechShield is to enable pro-Israeli investors to continue investing in top Israeli companies while protecting and optimizing their investments. We believe this is the best way to support Israel's tech industry. Investments drive the market effectively, contributing nearly 30% to Israel's GDP and ensuring the nation's prosperity.
•
TechShield is dedicated to preserving thousands of jobs and enhancing the resilience of the Israeli economy, particularly in challenging times.
•
We aim to extend each company's financial runway by 12-18 months, fostering strategic growth and stability.
•
TechShield values each company as a portfolio asset, providing full-scale support, including strategic guidance, network expansion, and resources to navigate economic uncertainties and achieve robust growth.
